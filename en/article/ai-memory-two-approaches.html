Claude's New Superpower: Interactive Visuals Right in Your Chat

2026-05-12 · ~3 min · Memory & RAG

Remember the days when explaining complex concepts required words and more words? Sometimes a picture really is worth a thousand words, and now, Claude gets that too. The AI assistant has leveled up its game, and it's bringing visuals right into your conversations.

Visuals That Pop Up Mid-Chat

Claude can now create interactive charts, diagrams, and visualizations right in the middle of your chat. No switching apps, no downloading separate files—just instant visuals that appear right where you need them. This feature is like bringing a whiteboard to your conversation with Claude. It started as a preview called "Imagine with Claude" last fall, and now it's available as a beta feature in regular chat conversations. The best part? You don't need to know a single line of code.

Two Types of Visuals: Artifacts vs. In-Line Visuals

To understand what makes this new feature special, let's compare it with Claude's existing artifact system:

FeatureIn-Line VisualsArtifacts
PurposeAid understanding during conversationCreate polished, shareable work
LocationAppears directly in chatShows in side panel
PersistenceTemporary (changes/disappears as chat evolves)Permanent until deleted
Best ForQuick explanations, interactive learningFinished documents, tools, reports

Think of in-line visuals as scratchpad drawings—perfect for exploring ideas—and artifacts as finished products ready to share.

See It in Action

Let's say you're trying to understand how compound interest works. Instead of just getting a paragraph explanation, Claude can now generate an interactive graph showing how your money grows over time. You can tweak the variables, see the curve change in real-time, and finally grasp that "eighth wonder of the world" Einstein was talking about. Or perhaps you're studying chemistry and ask about the periodic table. Claude can build an interactive version where you can click on elements to see their properties, electron configurations, and fun facts. It's like having a science teacher right in your chat window, ready with visual aids whenever you need them.

How to Use This Feature

The beauty of this update is its simplicity. The feature is on by default, so you don't need to hunt through settings to activate it. Claude will intelligently decide when a visual would help explain something better than words alone. If you want to be more direct, just ask: "Can you draw this as a diagram?" "Visualize how this might change over time" "Show me a graph of that data" Once Claude creates a visual, the conversation doesn't stop there. You can ask for adjustments, request more details, or dive deeper into any aspect. It's an interactive experience that evolves with your curiosity.

Part of a Bigger Picture

These interactive visuals aren't happening in a vacuum. They're part of a broader set of improvements to Claude's responses recently. Earlier this year, Claude began using specially designed formats for certain topics. Recipes now appear with ingredients and steps neatly organized, and weather queries come with visual forecasts. Claude has also gotten better at playing well with other apps. You can now interact directly with Figma, Canva, and Slack right within your discussions. It's becoming more of a digital Swiss Army knife every day.

Ready to Try?

The new interactive visualization feature is available on all plan types, so everyone can benefit from more visual conversations. Whether you're a student trying to grasp complex concepts, a professional looking to explain data clearly, or just someone who prefers learning visually, this update makes chatting with Claude more engaging and effective. Next time you're explaining or learning something complex, let Claude draw you a picture. You might be surprised how much easier it is to understand when you can see it come to life right before your eyes. Source: https://claude.com/blog/claude-builds-visuals
