Article 034 Agents Need Control Flow

2026-05-01 · ~1 min · Industry

Google DeepMind launched AlphaEvolve — a coding agent using Gemini models with evolutionary computation to automatically design new algorithms. It works in an iterative loop: generate code, test, evaluate, improve, and repeat. Already deployed at Google for data center optimization, chip design, and AI training acceleration. The self-improving loop is particularly notable — AlphaEvolve can optimize the training of the very models that power it.
